This blog is maintained by Vincent Driessen.  I'm a passionate developer
living in
"Deventer":http://maps.google.nl/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=nl&geocode=&q=deventer&sll=52.469397,5.509644&sspn=4.585004,7.591553&ie=UTF8&hq=&hnear=Deventer,+Gelderland&z=14
in the Netherlands.  I work as a software engineer at the IT department of the
"Dutch Tax and Customs Administration":http://www.belastingdienst.nl,
designing and building one of the coolest enterprise deployment tools using
nothing but open source tools, languages and frameworks.

I'm currently very interested in Python, Django, Ruby and JavaScript
development.  I have a background in Pascal/Delphi, PHP, C, C++, Java,
Haskell/Clean, C#/.NET, Objective-C/Cocoa and Jython (in chronological order).

Besides that, you may see me blog on (distributed) version control,
programming languages, parser/compiler design or the Vim editor.

Oh, and I love many things Apple creates.
